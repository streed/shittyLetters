How are the postcards mailed?
They are mailed via USPS and take about a business week to be printed and mailed. The timing depends on when you made your order or if there are weather or other types of delays like holidays.
Can I choose what it says on the postcard?
No, we don't want people to purposefully choose mean things and mail them to people...this would not be cool. So, we let mean things get chosen at random. If we receive a complaint about a specific phrase then we will consider taking it down.
